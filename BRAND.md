# FireHub Brand Guidelines

## Overview

This document provides guidance on the proper use of **FireHub brand assets**, including logos, colors, and icons.  
Adhering to these guidelines ensures **consistent branding** across documentation, presentations, and software integrations.

## Logo Usage

- Always use the official FireHub logo from the repository:
  [resources/graphics/logo/firehub.svg](https://github.com/The-FireHub-Project/the-firehub-project.github.io/tree/master/resources/graphics/logo)
- Do not stretch, distort, or recolor the logo.
- Maintain adequate **clear space** around the logo (at least 1x the logo height).

## Color Palette

- **Primary Color**: `#d50000`

These colors correspond to FireHub branding elements and support lifecycle icons.

## Icons

- Use icons from the official FireHub icon set:
  [resources/graphics/icons](https://github.com/The-FireHub-Project/the-firehub-project.github.io/tree/master/resources/graphics/icons)
- Do not modify, combine, or recolor icons outside the approved palette.

## Typography

- Recommended font: **Inter**, sans-serif fallback.
- Maintain legibility and hierarchy in headings, body text, and captions.

## Usage Examples

- Documentation headers
- Slide decks
- GitHub READMEs
- Marketing materials (with proper attribution)
