This document outlines the proper use and restrictions of FireHub trademarks.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the FireHub trademarks in the following contexts:

- **Referencing FireHub projects** in documentation, blogs, or presentations.
- **Attributing FireHub** to your software or research.
- **Educational or non-commercial purposes** that clearly reference The FireHub Project.

### Guidelines

- Always use the full name **“The FireHub Project”** on the first reference.
- Do not modify the FireHub logo or trademarked icons.
- Avoid using FireHub marks in a way that implies endorsement or partnership without explicit permission.
- Do not register FireHub marks in your own name.

## Prohibited Uses

- Using FireHub logos in commercial products without a proper license.
- Altering trademarks or logos in a misleading way.
- Implying affiliation or sponsorship with FireHub unless explicitly approved.
